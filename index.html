What We Offer

NACCIA, We Provide the synergy and impetus to move Nigeria Commercial farming to Industrial level improving value addition to the production from farms output to process plants to marketing it as finished industrial goods, thereby removing all the impediments that have put down the Nigeria farmer as a primary Raw materials producer.

off-takers
bulk processing
marketing
input sourcing

NACCIA Galvanizes and organises farmers and Agro processors from rural subsistence farmers to commercial and industrial ones to nestle around a federating farmer or the Central Farming Company owned by collective of all federating farms. They therefore cross guaranty each other thereby eliminating problems of impossible collaterals as a barrier that has impeded development of agriculture. Mobilize local Finance and Foreign Funding Capital to be accessed by the federating (Cooperative).

We Provide a platform for Nigerian farmers to meet a global minimum threshold for commercial success at the start of their agriculture ventures in terms of finance, expertise, off-taking and adding value for maximal benefits for all the agric value-chain
